Case Study: Analysis of a Telegram Phishing Attack

1. Executive Summary

  • Date: 28 February 2025
  • Target: Telegram users via compromised accounts.
  • Objective: Steal SMS verification codes to hijack accounts.
  • Outcome: Attack thwarted through vigilance, URL analysis, and domain reputation checks.

2. Incident Timeline

  1. Trigger: A message was received from a compromised friend's account:

    photos of you when you were child: http://touti1.icu/login"

  2. Suspicion: Identified red flags:
    • Used HTTP (unencrypted), not HTTPS.
    • Suspicious domain (touti1.icu) containing a '/login' path.
  3. Analysis:
    • Scanned the URL with VirusTotal (Trustwave flagged it as phishing).
    • Performed a WHOIS lookup to confirm the domain's age.
  4. Mitigation:
    • Avoided interaction with the link.
    • Alerted the compromised friend.

4. Lessons Learned

Key Takeaway: Newly registered domains (<7 days old) are high-risk indicators.

Skills Demonstrated:

  • Threat Hunting: Identified IoCs like HTTP and typosquatting.
  • OSINT: Analyzed domain registration and VirusTotal reports.
  • Incident Response: Reported the attack to protect others.
